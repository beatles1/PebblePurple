## PebblePurple
### Pebble Watchface
![Screenshot](https://appstore-api.repebble.com/api/assets/screenshots/6f06beee0a71423dba3bf84d/emery/13e33985-34d3-45e6-85e6-449bd7475e79.png)

Simple digital watchface with:

- Temperature from Open Meteo
- Bar showing battery level at bottom of screen
- Customsable colours (despite the name) as well as settings to disable temperature and date length
